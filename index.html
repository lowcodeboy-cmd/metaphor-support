Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tarot readings accurate?

Metaphor Tarot provides tarot readings for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only. The readings are generated by an intelligent semantic interpretation system and are designed to inspire personal insight. They should not be used as a basis for important life decisions. Always consult qualified professionals for medical, legal, financial, or psychological advice.

What languages does Metaphor Tarot support?

Metaphor Tarot currently supports English, Traditional Chinese, and Simplified Chinese. The app will automatically display in your device's preferred language if supported.

How do I clear my reading history?

You can clear all your data by going to Settings and selecting "Oblivion's Call". This will permanently delete all your reading history and chat records. Please note that this action cannot be undone.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. For refund requests, please contact:

  • Apple Support (for iOS purchases)
  • Google Play Support (for Android purchases)

Is my data secure?

Yes. Your reading history and chat data are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or share your personal reading data on our servers. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Please try the following steps:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store or Google Play
  • Restart your device

If the issue persists, please contact us with details about the problem.
